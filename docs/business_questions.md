# Business Questions

This project was designed to evaluate recruitment efficiency and workforce quality by integrating Applicant Tracking System (ATS) and Human Resource Information System (HRIS) data.

The analysis answers the following business questions:

---

## Executive Recruitment Overview

### 1. How many recruitment requisitions were created?

Measures the overall hiring demand during the analysis period.

---

### 2. How many requisitions were successfully filled?

Evaluates the organization's ability to close open positions.

---

### 3. What is the overall hiring rate?

Measures the percentage of recruitment requisitions that resulted in successful hires.

**Business Value**

Indicates overall recruitment effectiveness.

---

### 4. What is the average time required to fill a position?

Measures recruitment efficiency from requisition opening to successful hiring.

**Business Value**

Helps identify whether the hiring process is operating efficiently.

---

### 5. What is the overall offer acceptance rate?

Measures the percentage of extended offers accepted by candidates.

**Business Value**

Indicates employer attractiveness and recruitment process effectiveness.

---

### 6. What is the average candidate experience score?

Measures candidate satisfaction throughout the recruitment journey.

**Business Value**

Provides insight into employer branding and recruitment quality.

---

## Recruitment Performance

### 7. Which departments require the longest time to fill positions?

Compares recruitment efficiency across business departments.

**Business Value**

Helps HR prioritize recruitment resources for difficult-to-fill positions.

---

### 8. Which job levels require the longest hiring process?

Compares hiring duration across Entry, Mid, Senior, and Manager positions.

**Business Value**

Supports workforce planning and recruitment forecasting.

---

### 9. Which recruitment sources generate the highest hiring rate?

Compares hiring effectiveness across recruitment channels.

Examples include:

- Employee Referral
- LinkedIn
- Job Board
- Recruitment Agency
- Company Career Site
- University Career Fair
- Social Media

**Business Value**

Identifies the most productive sourcing channels.

---

### 10. Which departments achieve the highest offer acceptance rate?

Evaluates offer acceptance performance across departments.

**Business Value**

Helps identify departments that may require improvements in compensation, employer branding, or hiring processes.

---

## Candidate Experience

### 11. Does candidate experience differ across departments?

Measures average candidate experience by department.

**Business Value**

Identifies departments delivering better or worse recruitment experiences.

---

### 12. Which recruitment sources provide the best candidate experience?

Compares candidate satisfaction across recruitment channels.

**Business Value**

Evaluates the quality of each sourcing strategy beyond hiring volume.

---

### 13. Does hiring speed affect candidate experience?

Analyzes the relationship between average time to fill and candidate experience.

**Business Value**

Determines whether prolonged recruitment processes negatively impact candidate perception.

---

### 14. Does candidate experience influence offer acceptance?

Compares offer acceptance rates across Candidate Experience Groups.

Groups include:

- Poor
- Average
- Good
- Excellent

**Business Value**

Measures how recruitment quality influences hiring success.

---

### 15. Where do candidates exit the recruitment process?

Analyzes candidate distribution across recruitment stages.

Examples include:

- Screening
- Interview
- Offer Extended
- Hired
- Withdrawn
- Rejected

**Business Value**

Identifies major drop-off points in the recruitment pipeline.

---

## Workforce Analytics

### 16. What is the current workforce status?

Measures:

- Active Employees
- Turnover Rate
- Average Salary
- Average Employee Tenure

**Business Value**

Provides an executive summary of workforce health.

---

### 17. Which departments experience the highest employee turnover?

Compares turnover rates across departments.

**Business Value**

Helps HR identify departments requiring retention strategies.

---

### 18. Which recruitment sources produce employees with the best long-term retention?

Compares:

- Turnover Rate
- Average Employee Tenure

across recruitment sources.

**Business Value**

Evaluates long-term recruitment quality rather than simply hiring volume.

---

### 19. How does salary vary across job levels?

Compares average salary across:

- Entry
- Mid
- Senior
- Manager

**Business Value**

Supports compensation benchmarking and workforce planning.

---

### 20. Which recruitment sources produce employees with the longest tenure?

Compares average employee tenure by hiring channel.

**Business Value**

Identifies recruitment channels that consistently generate long-term employees.

---

# Expected Business Outcomes

This analysis enables HR leaders to:

- Improve recruitment efficiency
- Reduce Time to Fill
- Increase Offer Acceptance Rate
- Improve Candidate Experience
- Optimize recruitment channel investment
- Reduce employee turnover
- Increase long-term employee retention
- Support workforce planning with data-driven insights
